Lax Rotation-O-Matic

Instructions: Enter the names of 2 goalies, 6 starters for non-goalie positions, and optional bench players.
  • Player positions will be randomized, ensuring that the same player will not play the same position consecutively and attempting to give equal playing time to all players.
  • In the interest of giving equal playing time to all players, the bench after each "period" (midway and at the end of each quarter).
  • If there are 2 goalies, the first goalie play the first half as goalie and the other half starting on the bench. The second goalie will play the first half starting on the bench (being subbed in as necessary) and the second half as goalie.
  • There is an assumption that we have at least 7 players available for the game (1 goalie, 6 field)
